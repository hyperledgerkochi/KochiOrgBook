KochiOrgBook

Digital Trust Ecosystem project for the city of Kochi.

Learn more

KochiOrgBook(KOB) is a community initiative focused on building a digital ecosystem for the various associations in Kochi.







Our Stakeholders and what we provide for them

Associations in Kochi

  • Ease in proving your identity
  • Enabling them to host their digital services
  • Enable them to provide and validate digital credentials with ease
  • Associations who does not have the necessary infrastrcture in hosting a node can avail those services from our community cloud

Citizens in Kochi

  • Privacy
  • Empowerment to manage your identity
  • Validate the identity of an individual or associations within the jurisdiction with ease.
  • Avail digital services with ease.

Digital Service Providers

  • Provide capabilities in hosting digital services for the citizens of Kochi
  • Acessibility to all users of KochiOrgBook for offering digital services

Citizen Developers

  • Work towards enabling digital services
  • Earn valuable experience in blockchain projects

Our Key Projects

KOBPIU

KOB Public Identity Utility(KOBPIU) is our underlying blockchain network for the jurisdiction

KOBVON

KOBVON is a network of Verifiable Organisations registered with KOB.

KOBAW

KOB Association Wallet(KOBAW) is a decentralised wallet to store the VCs of associations registered with KOB.

KOBConnect

KOBConnect is the mobile wallet application where the citizens of Kochi can store their VCs.

KOBCC

KOB Community Cloud(KOBCC) is a dedicated cloud network for the associations to avail various hosted srevices.
