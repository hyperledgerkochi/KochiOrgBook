KochiOrgBook

Digital Trust Ecosystem project for the city of Kochi.

Learn more

KochiOrgBook(KOB) is a community initiative focused on building a digital ecosystem for the various associations in Kochi.







Our Stakeholders and what we provide for them

Associations in Kochi

  • Issue and verify digital credentials
  • Offer flexibility to host Association Wallet on community cloud
  • Onboard members and manage memberships thorugh credential exchange
  • Transparent and secure credential exchanges
  • Data protection and Privacy preserving by design and default
  • Openness and interoperability
  • Access to open-source libraries to manage the activities

Citizens in Kochi

  • Prove your identity using digital credential instead of paper
  • Privacy preserving
  • Share data safely and instantly
  • Complete control over your Digital Identity
  • Mobile wallet to manage digital credentials issued by associations
  • Secure access to digital services
  • Get access to trusted and verified data
  • P2P exchange of data
  • Prove your member with associations in an instant

Digital Service Providers

  • Unlock access to the citizens and associations in the jurisdiction
  • Make yourselves discoverable to a wider audiance
  • Provide secure digital services for the citizen and associations
  • Gain access to a platform to rapidly innovate and launch new digital services in a cost effective way
  • Increase the subscription for existing services

Citizen Developers

  • Build your skills and contribute back to the community
  • Work towards enabling digital services
  • Gain experience and build credibility
  • Gain opportunity to work for various Associations/DSP in the jurisdiction
  • Access to a real time development environment
  • Gain access to a community of experts
  • Chance to learn about different libraries and tools

Our Key Projects

KOBPIU

KOB Public Identity Utility(KOBPIU) is our underlying blockchain network for the jurisdiction

KOBVON

KOBVON is a network of Verifiable Organisations registered with KOB.

KOBAW

KOB Association Wallet(KOBAW) is a decentralised wallet to store the VCs of associations registered with KOB.

KOBConnect

KOBConnect is the mobile wallet application where the citizens of Kochi can store their VCs.

KOBCC

KOB Community Cloud(KOBCC) is a dedicated cloud network for the associations to avail various hosted srevices.
